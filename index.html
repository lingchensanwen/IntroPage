    My Research and Projects.

    April 2018 - July 2018

    CNN-Based Classification of Depression from Audios

    Advisor: Assocaite Professor Toshihiko Yamasaki

    I preprossed the data to get relative pure and confidential audio data. Then I transfered audios into spectrograms to get information and extract features from it. After that I optimized a Depression Classifer Model which was basically set with VGG19 structure. I did a poster presentation about this reasearch in MIRU2018.
