    My Research and Projects

    April 2018 - July 2018

    CNN-Based Classification of Depression from Audios

    Advisor: Associate Professor Toshihiko Yamasaki

    I preprossed the data and got relative pure and confidential audio data. Then I transfered audios into spectrograms to get information and extract features from it. After that I optimized a Depression Classifer Model which was basically set with VGG19 structure. It improved recall scores by over 15%, and got better result at ROC curve and F1 scores either. I submitted a paper and did a poster presentation about this reasearch in MIRU2018.

    2018, 2017

    Course projects

    Lead Developer

    I wrote a colored Tetris Game with different levels, as the level goes higher, the blocks will fall faster. I also refined a ICA project that could separate a song mixed by Turkish march and To Alice as well as an image formed by the overlap of Lena and Elina withouth using FastICA tools.

    December 2016

    TI CUP Intelligent Car Contest held by DUT

    Test Developer

    With the knowledge of usage of 51 single-chip microcomputer, I completed the design of an electronic intelligent car, with differential control system. After assembling the car, I wrote C codes and burn the program into the car to control its operation. Having refined it over and over, finally, we got the second place in the car race (2/63).

    December 2016, 2015 and 2014

    TI CUP Electronic Design Contest held by DUT

    Lead Designer

    In 2014 and 2015 contest, I designed and tuned a mini amplifer that could amplify music. In 2016 contest, I co-worked a smart flower pot which could automatically water itself and turn to sun. Our smart pot won first prize in 2016 TI Cup among 150 teams(Top 10%).
